What it's like to work with me

Since working with Kai, I feel like I have been empowered to an even greater degree. Normally Product Managers are seen as the "Force Multiplier" for the business, but I feel like working with Kai has been the opposite. Kai doesn't shy away from a meeting or conversation with anyone, and always asks great clarifying questions. They will even go so far as to scope out tickets for me and lead the team through heavy technical discussions, and have saved me so much time and allowed me to focus efforts elsewhere that I have had to put off. To me, this serves as a great example of a leader, as they are working to help people outside the confines of those they collaborate with on a daily basis. Kai also maintains an EXTREMELY positive attitude about seemingly everything. When things are shit, and everyone is stressed, Kai always finds a way to keep themselves positive, and focused on the things that are going well. I feel that this kind of attitude becomes contagious, and it forces people to do the same, and I even find myself feeling a little more positive knowing I have Kai on my side coming into work, and things won't be so bad as a result. Lastly, Kai feels like a good person, more than just a co-worker. I never feel anxiety or any negative emotions whenever I get a DM from Kai, and I'm always excited to answer because I know Kai is either going to have an insightful thought/question/idea about work, or be willing to joke around to lighten the mood, and banter a bit back and forth, but never crosses any boundaries or lines.
